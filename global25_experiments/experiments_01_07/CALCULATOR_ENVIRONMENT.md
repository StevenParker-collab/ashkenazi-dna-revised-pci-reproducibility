## Output handling

Calculator labels, distances, and displayed coefficients are preserved under `results/raw/`. Experiment 1 uses TSV because that is the full export format supplied. Context transcriptions and supplemental matched runs are stored separately under `results/context/`. A source omitted from a displayed table is treated only as a zero coefficient in that run, not as proof of zero historical ancestry.
