EXPERIMENT 14 — REGIONAL ASHKENAZI FULL MODERN PANEL WITH LEVANT CONTROLS

Purpose

The identical full modern panel from Experiment 10 is applied to ten named
regional Ashkenazi population averages. No individual is used. The series
tests whether the pooled result is stable across western and eastern
Ashkenazi averages and whether later European displacement changes the older
Italki and central Mediterranean signal.
