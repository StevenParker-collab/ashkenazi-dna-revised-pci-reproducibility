EXPERIMENT 15 — IRON AGE SOUTHERN LEVANT CONTINUITY MODEL

Purpose

Three Iron Age coastal southern-Levantine population averages are modeled
against pre-Iron-Age Levantine site-period averages plus transparent Mycenaean
and Anatolian Bronze Age composite averages.

Composite construction

Composite_Greece_LBA_Mycenaean_7_site_averages is an unweighted mean of seven
predeclared qualifying site averages.

Composite_Turkey_Anatolia_EBA_4_site_averages is an unweighted mean of four
predeclared qualifying site averages.

The composites are not selected individuals.
